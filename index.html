Welcome to Student Living, your number one resource for finding housing as a student!

Currently located at Bishop's University, University of Toronto, and Western University.

Trying to find housing for any student is a challenge. But with Student Living its a one stop shop. with the ability to search, select, and then message respective landlords and what they are offering, students finally don't have to worry about finding somewhere to live. Instead of word of mouth being the main means that students get contact info for landlords, Student Living fills the void in property service.
